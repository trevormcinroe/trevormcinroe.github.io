Scoring and How to Win

Victory Conditions

Civitas supports all major victory types from Civilization V, each requiring different strategic approaches:

Science Victory

Build the spaceship parts and launch to Alpha Centauri. Requires heavy investment in science infrastructure and specific technologies.

Domination Victory

Capture all enemy capitals. Demands military might, strategic positioning, and efficient conquest campaigns.

Cultural Victory

Achieve influential status over all civilizations through tourism and cultural policies.

Diplomatic Victory

Win the United Nations vote by maintaining strong relationships and city-state alliances.

Scoring System

The scoring system evaluates multiple dimensions of civilization development such a city growth, technological progress, world wonder and great work production, and much more. While Civilization's built-in scoring system corresponds to ...., it is not directly correlated to progress towards winning the game via one of the aforementioned victory conditions. This is similar to a game of chess giving a player points for developing pieces.
